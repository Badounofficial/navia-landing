Gentle answers

What women often ask before joining

Everything below is written the way we'd want it whispered to us. With honesty, care, and no pressure.

Is Navia a replacement for my doctor?
No, and we'll never pretend to be. Navia is a companion, not a clinician. She won't diagnose, prescribe, or replace any professional you already trust. Think of her as the space between appointments, where you can be heard when no one else is listening.
How does Navia protect what I share?
Your words belong to you. Everything you share is encrypted, never sold, and never used to train models without your clear, explicit consent. You can review, export, or delete anything, any time, without having to explain why.
Will Navia text me all day?
Only when you want her to. You choose the rhythm. A morning check-in, a quiet evening note, or silence for a week. Navia follows your pace, never the other way around. Presence, not pressure, is the whole point.
I'm pregnant (or trying, or postpartum). Does Navia adapt?
Yes. Navia recognizes every chapter. Trying to conceive, pregnancy week by week, postpartum, your cycle, perimenopause. She gently shifts her tone, her questions, and what she notices, so she meets you exactly where you are today.
Can Navia really help with how I'm feeling emotionally?
That's her heart. Navia listens without judgment, reflects what she hears back to you with warmth, and reminds you that nothing you feel is "too much." For heavier moments, she will always, gently, encourage you to reach out to a trusted person or professional.
What exactly is the gifting part, and is it shopping?
It isn't shopping. When someone you love is going through a hard week, a milestone, or the long days of morning sickness, Navia helps you send a thoughtful gift curated for that exact moment. No carts, no ads, no endless scrolling. Just presence, delivered.
If I send a gift, does the other person see my private notes?
Never. Only the gift itself, and whatever message you choose to write, are shared. Her mood entries, her diary, her check-ins all stay hers alone. Navia never shares what isn't hers to share.
Can I stop or pause whenever I want?
Always. There's no lock-in, no guilt-trip, no pressure. If you need to pause for a week, pause. If you want to leave, leave, and take everything you shared with you. Navia is here on your terms, or not at all.
How is Navia different from the wellness apps I already have?
Most apps track you. Navia walks with you. You don't count calories or close rings. You share what's real, and she responds with warmth, memory, and care. She remembers the little things. She asks how last week felt. She's less of a tool, more of a presence.
What will it cost, and do I have to pay now?
You don't pay anything today. The first 500 members receive early access for free, plus a surprise on launch. When we do introduce pricing, it will be simple, transparent, and honest. No hidden fees, no dark patterns, ever.

Still unsure? That's okay. Navia is built for women who take their time.
