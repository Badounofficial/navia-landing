# Ozaia: 4 Ultra-Realistic Photo Prompts

*Designed for Midjourney v6.1 `--style raw --s 100` or Flux Pro 1.1. Maximum photorealism.*

---

## Visual Coherence Rules (apply to all 4)

- **Moon**: NASA full moon, warm ivory-golden tone (not white), Mare Tranquillitatis visible, Tycho crater ray system, subtle amber limb glow
- **Color palette**: indigo (#2B2940), warm gold (#E8C58F), soft rose (#E8A89D), cream (#F5F0E6)
- **Camera**: Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.4 (except P4: 50mm f/1.2), ISO 800-1600
- **Film stock feel**: Kodak Portra 400, warm 5800K, subtle grain, soft contrast
- **Post-processing**: lifted shadows, contained highlights, gentle film grain, no HDR

## Prompt 1 — NASA Full Moon

```
Ultra high resolution photograph of a full moon against a pure black night sky, exactly matching NASA Apollo mission imagery. The moon surface shows extreme detail: Mare Tranquillitatis, Mare Serenitatis, and Mare Imbrium clearly visible as dark basaltic plains. Tycho crater at the bottom with bright ray system extending across the surface. Copernicus crater sharply defined with terraced walls and central peak. The moon has a warm ivory-golden tone, not clinical white, with a subtle amber glow along the limb suggesting atmospheric refraction. Surface texture shows thousands of small craters, mountains casting sharp shadows along the terminator edge. Color temperature slightly warm at 5800K. The surrounding sky is absolute black with no stars to maintain NASA reference accuracy. Shot with a Celestron EdgeHD 14-inch telescope with Canon EOS R5 body at prime focus, ISO 400, 1/250s exposure. No chromatic aberration, no atmospheric distortion, tack sharp across the entire disc. The image feels scientific yet beautiful, documentary yet intimate. 8K resolution, no text, no watermark --ar 1:1 --style raw --s 100 --v 6.1
```

## Technical Notes

- **Midjourney**: Use `--style raw --s 100` for maximum photorealism. Avoid `--s` above 300.
- **Alternative**: Flux Pro 1.1 (via Replicate) currently produces the most photorealistic AI images, especially for faces and skin textures.
- **Coherence**: All 4 prompts share the same moon description, color palette, camera system, and film stock feel.
- **Upscaling**: Generate at default resolution, then upscale 2x before final use.
