## 4. Founder Photo

**File name:** `founder.jpg`
**Dimensions:** 400×400 px minimum (will display at 160×160 in a circle)
**This one cannot be AI-generated — it must be a real photo of Sebastien.**

### Photo guidelines:
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders, slightly off-center
- **Expression:** Warm, confident, approachable — slight smile
- **Background:** Clean, neutral (white wall, soft blur, or outdoor natural light)
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred, or soft studio light — no harsh shadows
- **Attire:** Smart casual or professional (dark shirt or blazer works well)
- **Format:** Square crop or portrait that can be cropped to a circle
- **Quality:** High resolution, sharp focus on the face
- **Mood:** The photo should say "I care about your well-being" — not corporate, not gym-bro

### Quick options if you don't have a pro photo:
1. iPhone portrait mode against a white/light wall, near a window (golden hour = best)
2. Ask someone to take it — selfies don't convey the same authority
3. Edit with a free tool like remove.bg for background cleanup
